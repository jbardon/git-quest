Get help for your Quest

Get help

This page gathers help pages for all quests.

If you’re stuck in a quest, you can use git-quest help. The help page related to your quest will open automatically.

Help pages are divides into two parts: short answer and step by step solution. The former is the list of commands to complete the quest. While the latter includes command results and explanations for beginners.
